Pastor Brendt Wills has authored books designed to help believers grow in their faith, find freedom, and walk in the power of God. These resources come from years of ministry experience and a heart to see people transformed.

The Weekly Reset

by Brendt Wills

The Weekly Reset

Published January 2023 62 Pages Hardcover

Have you ever had one of those weeks? You know, everything goes wrong! It seems like you've hurt or let down everyone closest to you. Wouldn't it be amazing if God had set a mechanism in order to reset the whole thing?

Well, then this book is for you. The Weekly Reset explores the Biblical principle of rest and renewal, helping readers understand how God designed us to find restoration and start fresh each week.
