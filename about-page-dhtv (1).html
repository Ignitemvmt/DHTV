OUR MISSION

David Heavener TV is a fresh alternative to Hollywood's immoral programming. We deliver Truth without compromise through compelling stories and investigative content that mainstream media refuses to touch.

As an exclusive faith-based network, we examine current events through a biblical lens, offering bold, thought-provoking content for believers seeking deeper understanding on topics often overlooked by traditional churches.

DAVID'S STORY

David Heavener is a seasoned Hollywood filmmaker who has written, directed, produced and starred in over 50 motion pictures. He has directed Academy Award winners and nominees including Martin Landau, Tony Curtis and many others. His movies have premiered on HBO, Showtime and major TV networks.

Throughout a career in Hollywood, David encountered victims of demonic attacks, SRA, trafficking, programming, and the New Age. This experience led him into creating David Heavener Ministries, focusing on exposing these dark forces through TV and film.

DAVID'S TESTIMONY

"Though I served many years as a music minister, I led a separate life as a movie producer. Finally, things reached a crisis and God brought me to the bottom, then re-created me."

"The change that resulted was that not only did I bring Jesus into Hollywood, I realized that God had commissioned me to bring filmmaking into the church."

Though he could've gone to the studios to get financed, he didn't want to give up editorial control which would have watered down the project. Consequently, because he brought Christ into the media marketplace, industry business associates rejected him. Additionally, the apostate church now rejects him for the controversial topics he tackles. This has only deepened his resolve to deliver Truth Uncompromised.
